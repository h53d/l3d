JT File Viewer Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 03, 2026

Quick answer

JT File Viewer does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. Your JT files, screenshots, recordings, and exports are processed offline on your device. The app keeps recent-file access data locally so you can reopen files and assemblies more easily.

Policy

Does JT File Viewer collect personal data?

JT File Viewer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or third-party analytics services. Viewing, inspection, and export happen locally on your device.

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking
  • No cloud uploads of JT files or derived meshes
  • No data sharing with us or others

Your JT assemblies, screenshots, recordings, and exports remain under your control unless you choose to share them yourself.

Does JT File Viewer upload JT files to the cloud?

No. JT import, product-structure inspection, viewport rendering, screenshots, screen recording, and STL or GLB export run entirely offline on your Mac, iPhone, iPad, or Apple Vision device. We do not upload your files to any server.

What data does JT File Viewer store on my device?

Opened JT files are processed on your local device. JT File Viewer stores recent-file information and local file access data so you can reopen recently used JT files and multi-file assemblies without sending data off-device. These records stay on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Does JT File Viewer use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs?

No. We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party data services. There is no tracking, fingerprinting, or hidden network calls for viewer functionality.

How are purchases and subscriptions handled?

JT File Viewer purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. JT File Viewer can verify purchase entitlement status through Apple StoreKit APIs, but we do not receive or store payment card details, billing addresses, or App Store account credentials.

What are my privacy rights when using JT File Viewer?

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for you to request, delete, or revoke from us. Your intellectual property and CAD visualization data remain yours alone. You can clear recent-file history and app-managed recent-file access data from within the app at any time.

How will JT File Viewer notify me of privacy policy changes?

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

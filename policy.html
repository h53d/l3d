Lite3D: JT File Viewer Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 01, 2026

Quick answer

Lite3D: JT File Viewer does not collect, store, or transmit personal data. Your JT files, screenshots, recordings, and exports are processed offline on your device. The app only keeps a local recent-files list and assembly folder bookmarks in its sandbox.

Policy

Does Lite3D collect personal data?

Lite3D: JT File Viewer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or third-party analytics services. Viewing, inspection, and export happen locally on your device.

  • No user accounts or sign-in
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking
  • No cloud uploads of JT files or derived meshes
  • No data sharing with us or others

Your JT assemblies, screenshots, recordings, and exports remain under your control unless you choose to share them yourself.

Does Lite3D upload JT files to the cloud?

No. JT import, product-structure inspection, viewport rendering, screenshots, screen recording, and STL or GLB export run entirely offline on your Mac, iPhone, iPad, or Apple Vision device. We do not upload your files to any server.

What data does Lite3D store on my device?

Opened JT files are processed in memory and on your local device. Lite3D only stores a local recent-files list and, when you grant folder access for multi-file assemblies, security-scoped bookmarks so repeat opens can access sibling part files without sending data off-device. These records stay in your app sandbox and are not transmitted to us.

Does Lite3D use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs?

No. We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or third-party data services. There is no tracking, fingerprinting, or hidden network calls for viewer functionality.

What are my privacy rights when using Lite3D?

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for you to request, delete, or revoke from us. Your intellectual property and CAD visualization data remain yours alone. You can clear recent-file history from within the app at any time.

How will Lite3D notify me of privacy policy changes?

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be noted by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
